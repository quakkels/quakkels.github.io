5 (Persistence)

Overhead Press Day

Decided to switch up the set and rep scheme just a little. I’ve been on a calorie deficit for about a week, and I think I’ll be able to keep up with my lifts if I reduce the reps per set, but then increase total sets.

Superset 1

Lift Weight Sets Reps
Press 155 5 3
Lat Pulldown 155 5 6
Jumping Jacks bw 5 20

Superset 2

Lift Weight Sets Reps
SSB Squat 285 4 3
DB Seated Press 50 4 8
50’ DB Suitcase Carry 80 4 1

This new rep/set configuration worked well. Kept rest to only 90 seconds for the first superset, and only about 2:30 minutes for the second superset. With warmups and cleaning up I was done in about an hour and five minutes.
